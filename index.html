Gameplay

Welcome to "Soon Rich"!

Game Rules

The objective of this game is to collect bottles and exchange them for coins.
With the coins, you can purchase food.
The more you eat, the more time you gain until you take a golden shot. Important note: You can eat beyond "100% Food".
You can also party. Doing so earns you experience and allows you to level up. Partying is exhausting, so be sure not to starve while partying.

Here are the basic steps to play the game:

Game Screen and Buttons:

Experience and Level:
At the top of the screen, you'll see your experience and level.

Bottles:
Initially, you need to collect bottles.

Coins:
You can exchange bottles for coins.

Food:
You can purchase food using coins. The red bar indicates how full you are.

Countdown:
Below, you'll see a countdown. This indicates the time left until the "Golden Shot".

Items:
You can discover items that provide multipliers for bottles or coins. These items must be activated.

Game Buttons:

"Collect":
Tap on the bottle icon to collect bottles and earn experience points. Beware of dangers that can deplete your health.
"Change":
Tap on the coin icon to exchange coins for other resources.
"Get Food":
Tap on the fast-food icon to obtain food and refill your food meter. Even when you're full, each food item grants an extra +1 second before taking the Golden Shot.
"Party Hard":
Tap on the party icon to receive special rewards. Beware of dangers that can deplete your health.
"Story":
Tap on the book icon to learn the backstory of the game.
Game Objective:

Accumulate experience, level up, earn coins, stock up on food, and achieve all possible rewards. The goal is to become as rich as possible and conquer the game's challenges.
Enjoy playing "Soon Rich by Codepec"!
